Spruiten met Zwarte Look

Description

Origineel recept: van Ottolenghi

Ingredients

  • 450g Brussels sprouts, trimmed and cut in half lengthways (400g)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¾ tsp caraway seeds
  • 20g (about 12), roughly chopped
  • 2 tbsp picked thyme leaves
  • 30g unsalted butter
  • 30g pumpkin seeds, toasted
  • 1½ tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C fan.
  2. Mix the sprouts with the oil and ¼ teaspoon of salt, then spread out on a parchment-lined baking tray. Roast for 10 minutes, until the sprouts are golden-brown but still crunchy.
  3. Meanwhile, lightly crush the caraway seeds with a pestle and mortar. Add the and thyme and crush them to form a rough paste.
  4. Put the butter into a large sauté pan and place on a medium high heat. Cook for 3 minutes, until melted and dark brown. Add the crushed garlic paste, sprouts, pumpkin seeds and 1/8 teaspoon of salt. Stir for 30 seconds, then take off the heat. Stir in the lemon juice and transfer to a large bowl or individual plates. Drizzle over the and serve at once.
